Alignmentaj tool was created in 2021 in Laboratory of the Mosaic of Autoimmunity. Alignmentaj in the input accepts the amino acid sequences of two proteins.

Our tool dissecte the primary amino acid sequences first protein into pentapeptides (if you choose number 5). The obtained pentapeptides are compared to the amino acid

sequences of the second protein. In the results, you can see similar peptides in the proteins analyzed. For example:
